# Mission

**Learner:** Ollie

**Why this topic:** I run a business and want to understand how central-bank interest-rate
decisions ripple into borrowing costs, demand, and my own planning — well enough to read
the financial news with real comprehension rather than headline-level intuition.

**Success looks like:** I can explain, in my own words, what a base rate is and why a rate
change takes months to bite — and I'd trust myself to reason about a new rate announcement.

**Scope:** Knowledge-based understanding only. Not financial advice, not trading.
