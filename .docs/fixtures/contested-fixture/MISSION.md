# Mission

**Learner:** Ollie

**Why this topic:** I want to understand why different central banks seem to make such different
decisions — the Federal Reserve and the Bank of England often respond differently to the same
global shock. Understanding their mandates explains a lot of that divergence.

**Success looks like:** I can explain what a "central bank mandate" is, describe the difference
between a dual mandate and a single mandate, and understand why serious economists defend each
approach with Tier 1/2 evidence.

**Scope:** Institutional understanding of mandate design. Not financial advice, not investment
guidance.
