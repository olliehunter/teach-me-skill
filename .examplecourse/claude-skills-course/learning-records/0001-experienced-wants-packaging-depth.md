# Learner is experienced and wants depth, including packaging

Ollie is technically experienced and explicitly asked to skip hand-holding and go to depth,
including the advanced packaging story (MCPB and plugins). The course is therefore paced for a
practitioner: it states field constraints and edge cases directly rather than motivating basics
at length, and it treats the Skills vs. Plugins vs. MCPB distinction as a first-class outcome.

**Implications:** Future sessions can go straight into hands-on authoring (e.g. drafting a real
SKILL.md, writing an MCPB manifest.json, or scaffolding a Claude Code plugin) without re-teaching
the conceptual model in [[MISSION.md]]. If the learner wants to *build* rather than learn, hand off
to the actual tooling — `mcpb init`/`pack`, `claude plugin init` — and the communities in
RESOURCES.md.
